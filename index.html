The Pioneers of Artificial Intelligence

An AI Pioneer is a visionary whose ideas and discoveries fundamentally shaped the field of artificial intelligence. These brilliant minds—from ancient mathematicians who conceived of algorithms to modern computer scientists who build neural networks—laid the groundwork for the intelligent systems we see today. They challenged the limits of human knowledge, dared to ask "Can machines think?", and built the conceptual and practical tools to begin answering that question. Exploring their stories is to understand the very foundations of AI.
